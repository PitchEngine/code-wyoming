Climbing Destinations

I love travel, climbing, and most of all, putting those things together. So lucky me, I've gone climbing in quite a few exotic locales, including...

Thailand

I've been to Thailand twice now. If you plan on going, stick to the shade and drink plenty of H 2O.

Japan

I lived in Japan for a bit there, and managed to get out and climb some amazing places.

I kept a blog of all my time in Japan. You can check it out on my site

England

Quarries under grey skies.

France

France... was scary.

We did most of our climbing in the Verdon Gorge, where a wrong move would give you twenty seconds of freefall to think about what you did wrong before, well, you know.

Sweden

I managed to squeeze in an afternoon trip to the crags in far north Sweden, as we as an even session at a fantastic gymin Halmstad.
